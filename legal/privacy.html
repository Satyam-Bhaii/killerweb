Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, billing address
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party providers
  • Usage Data: Server logs, IP addresses, browser type
  • Support Data: Ticket communications, chat logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process payments and billing
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (payment processors, analytics, support tools) who assist in operating our business. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and for authentication. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, we will remove personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our website.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@killernodes.com.
