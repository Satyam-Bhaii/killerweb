Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services.

Home / Refund Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new customers on their first service. If you're not satisfied with our hosting for any reason, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase — no questions asked.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • You must be a first-time customer
  • The request must be made within 7 days of the original purchase
  • Your account must not have violated our Terms of Service
  • Refund requests must be submitted via our support ticket system

Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Dedicated IP addresses
  • Domain registrations
  • Add-on services (extra storage, etc.)
  • Services beyond the initial 7-day period
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Cryptocurrency payments (due to transaction fees)

How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your account at panel.killernodes.xyz
  2. Navigate to the Support section
  3. Open a new ticket with the subject "Refund Request"
  4. Include your order number and reason for the refund

Processing Time

Refund requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, the refund will be issued to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear in your account.

Partial Refunds

For upgrades or downgrades made after the 7-day period, we may offer pro-rated account credits at our discretion. These credits can be applied to future services but are not refundable as cash.

Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback without first contacting our support team may result in immediate account termination and a ban from future services. Please reach out to us first — we're happy to resolve any issues.

Contact

If you have questions about our Refund Policy, please contact billing@killernodes.com.
