Service Level Agreement

Our commitment to uptime and reliability.

Home / SLA

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Uptime Guarantee

KillerNodes guarantees 99.9% network uptime for all game server hosting services. This applies to the availability of our network infrastructure and control panel access.

SLA Credit Structure

If we fail to meet our uptime guarantee, you may be eligible for service credits as follows:

Monthly Uptime Credit Percentage
99.0% – 99.9%10% of monthly fee
95.0% – 98.9%25% of monthly fee
90.0% – 94.9%50% of monthly fee
Below 90.0%100% of monthly fee

Exclusions

The following are NOT covered under this SLA:

  • Scheduled maintenance (announced at least 24 hours in advance)
  • Emergency security patches
  • Customer-initiated actions (misconfiguration, resource overuse)
  • Third-party service outages (DDoS attacks beyond our mitigation capacity)
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, war, etc.)
  • Issues with customer's ISP or local network

How to Claim Credits

  1. Submit a support ticket within 7 days of the incident
  2. Include the date, time, and duration of the outage
  3. Provide any relevant logs or screenshots
  4. Our team will verify the claim and apply credits within 5 business days

Credit Limitations

  • Credits are applied to future invoices only (no cash refunds)
  • Maximum credit per month cannot exceed 100% of that month's fee
  • Credits do not roll over to subsequent months
